Submission Process

Manuscripts must be submitted online using the Microsoft Conference Management Toolkit (CMT). Authors need to set up a CMT account, input paper details (abstract, authors, tracks), and upload the blind PDF file.

Microsoft CMT Portal

Submissions are managed securely through Microsoft CMT. Ensure all co-authors are registered inside the portal and that email IDs match their academic credentials.

Formatting Guidelines

Submissions must be compiled using standard templates. To avoid editorial screening rejection, make sure author details are not written in initial blind reviews.

Download Templates

Post-Acceptance Steps

Upon receiving paper acceptance, authors must upload final camera-ready files, complete reviewer revisions, sign the publication copyright agreement, and pay early bird registration fees to confirm proceedings listings.
