Transportation & Travel Information

By Air (Pune Airport)

Pune International Airport (PNQ) is approximately 18 km from the campus. Taxis and app-based cabs (Uber/Ola) are easily available.

By Rail (Pune Station)

Pune Junction Railway Station is about 9 km from the university. Regular local buses and auto-rickshaws connect to the Satara Road campus.

Local Auto-Rickshaws

Pune has extensive local bus transit (PMPML) and auto-rickshaws. Mention 'Bharati Vidyapeeth Dhankawadi Campus' to drivers.
