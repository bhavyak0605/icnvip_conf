Publication Ethics and Copyright

ICNVIP-2027 operates under guidelines structured by the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE). Any duplicate submissions or unethical publication actions trigger immediate paper retraction.

Prior to publishing, authors must sign a Copyright Transfer Form, giving publishing permissions to Springer. Author consent statements are completed electronically during camera-ready uploads.
