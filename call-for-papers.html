Submission Overview

ICNVIP-2027 invites academic researchers, scientists, and engineers to submit their original technical manuscripts for peer-review. Submissions must present novel scientific contributions and must not be under review elsewhere.

Scope & Tracks

The conference covers a broad scientific array across Nanoelectronics (Quantum spin models), VLSI design (Analog and Mixed-signal circuits), and Signal/Image analysis frameworks.

Explore Program Tracks

Formatting Guidelines

All submissions must fit the template models. Initial papers must be submitted without author names (double-blind review mechanism) with a maximum count of 6 pages.

Download Paper Template

Plagiarism and Review Criteria

  • All papers are scanned using official anti-plagiarism tools (similarity index must not exceed 15%).
  • Peer-review comments will guide corrections, managed by the Technical Program Committee.
  • Final camera-ready papers require a signed copyright form for official book publication.
