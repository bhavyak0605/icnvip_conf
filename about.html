Bharati Vidyapeeth

Deemed to be University

Bharati Vidyapeeth, established in 1964, is a highly acclaimed educational institution in India. Founded by Dr. Patangrao Kadam, the organization has pioneered high-standards learning paradigms across multiple disciplines.

With a prestigious 'A+' grade NAAC accreditation, the university supports advanced research programs, premium infrastructure facilities, and extensive social-inclusion developments.

College of Engineering

Pune, India

Established in 1983, Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering, Pune is a premier engineering college ranking high in NIRF evaluations. It promotes research in key science and computing arenas.

The campus houses digital fabrication cells, incubation units, and corporate alliances, transforming students into industry-ready leaders and tech startup founders.

Department of ECE

Electronics & Communication

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering drives technical expertise in VLSI, device modeling, wireless networks, signal processors, and machine vision.

Equipped with computational workstations and industry-standard design tools, the department acts as a center of expertise for research publications and technological developments.

About ICNVIP-2027

The Conference Vision

ICNVIP-2027 aims to bridge academia and global industry by showcasing original peer-reviewed studies. It covers advancements in TFET devices, hardware security, AI accelerators, and signal processing.

The two-day conference features parallel technical tracks, plenary keynote panels, and interactive poster forums where experts and young scholars establish long-term research ties.
